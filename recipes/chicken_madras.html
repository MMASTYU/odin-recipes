CHICKEN MADRAS

Description

Ditch the takeaway menu and cook our healthy chicken madras curry instead. This simple family dinner is full of fragrant spices and tender pieces of chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 onion, peeled and quartered
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • thumb-sized chunk of ginger, peeled
  • 1/2 chilli
    • 1 tbsp vegetable oil tsp turmeric 1 tsp ground cumin 1 tsp ground coriander 1-2 tsp hot chilli powder (depending on how spicy you like your curry) 4 chicken breasts, cut into chunks 400g can chopped tomatoes small pack coriander, chopped rice, naan and mango chutney, to serve
